The Primary Taboo—
Do not walk the way back.
Even if you do,
do not step onto your footprints.
They belong  
to broken brick and drying cement,  
to earth clotted and parched,  
to stones half-buried,  
to sand consumed  
and waters consuming.

They belong
to concrete.
