<pre style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, 'Nimbus Roman No9 L', 'KaiTi', 'STKaiti', 'SimSun', serif; font-size: 1.15rem; line-height: 1.9; background: none; border: none; padding: 0; margin: 1em 0;">
Spork oh spork,
How you troubled us dorks!
Can you be a piece 
of motionless meat,
boiled, spoiled, wasted on the street?
Some say otherwise,
the answer couldn't be that creative:
All humans have the same mind,
although by culture we bind.
Language is still within guessing range,
let's make a reasonable change;
I say it's short for sport fork—
Are you deranged?
And me, my friends, know the true answer—
an answer plain and boring,
unimaginative but scoring.
But what's the fun in revealing,
when we all know the debating
is nothing but self-comforting.
