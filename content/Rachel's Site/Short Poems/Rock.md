I, unrest filled.
Days are blurring into one another—
the current takes me
to a kingdom unknown
but awaiting as destined.
If only I had an anchor—
if only I can...
be a rock amidst the stream.

Albeit in perplexity,
I shall wait,
observing patiently,
until the fog thins
and the horizon breathes again.
But now,
I drift blind,
staring through mist and into more mist,
lost inside the losing.

被不安填满的我
终日惶惶
只能顺流而下
漂向未知的既定终点
要是有支锚该多好呀
我可以做一块河中顽石
即便混沌彷徨
至少还在原地
等待着迷雾散开
再启程去往前方
而不是像现在这样
糊里糊涂
雾里看雾
