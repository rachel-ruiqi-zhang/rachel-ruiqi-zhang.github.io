<pre style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, 'Nimbus Roman No9 L', 'KaiTi', 'STKaiti', 'SimSun', serif; font-size: 1.15rem; line-height: 1.9; background: none; border: none; padding: 0; margin: 1em 0;">
Grids between me and the world
Keyboard stutters;
water thickens into mud.
Can the wind still howl and swirl?

I crack my bones open
and lay bare
blood, flesh, and tendons—
unspooling.

Finally, 
I am free to flow.

我和世界隔着格子
键盘阻涩
水是泥淖
风会吹不动吗
把骨头掰开
血和肉和筋展开
铺开
我终于能流动了
