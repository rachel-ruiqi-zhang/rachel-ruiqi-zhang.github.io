<pre style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, 'Nimbus Roman No9 L', 'KaiTi', 'STKaiti', 'SimSun', serif; font-size: 1.15rem; line-height: 1.9; background: none; border: none; padding: 0; margin: 1em 0;">
Rain spent, sky yet to clear—
Fish-belly white rippling above the sun.
The world now belongs
to fallen petals
and readied buds.
Damp earth tingles with a salty tang.
Within grows pine needles too tough to chew,
roots buried deep—
flowering bushes, dead trunks, evergreens,
wild grass, dry grass:
every season layered,
every season present.
I tread on the dew
stumbling into a bush of golden thorns—
golden shrine.
O orange-beaked black bird,
To hold a grudge
is also to hold on to the past.
From clouded skies to clear light,
the castle in the heavens leads the way.
Heavy clouds pump its heart;
The wind runs, breathing itself,
across the pier.



雨过天未晴
西方泛起鱼肚白
世界属于零落的花与待萌的新芽
带着腥气的泥土里
长着嚼不动的针叶
埋着花树枯树常青树
野草枯草
春夏秋冬
我踩着露水钻进金黄的荆棘树
还是黄金屋
橘嘴的乌鸫呀
记仇也是一种念旧
从阴天到晴天
天上的城堡指着路
浓云是流动的心脏
风在栈桥上奔跑
