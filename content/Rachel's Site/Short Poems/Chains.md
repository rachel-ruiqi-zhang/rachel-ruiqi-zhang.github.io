Behind me, a chain—
binding/leading to my past.
Scattered shards
piling into a white silence,
crushing the footprints,
and staining themselves with dust.

我身后有一条锁链 
捆着/通往我的过去
杂乱的碎片
白茫茫地堆起
碾过脚印
也沾上了灰
