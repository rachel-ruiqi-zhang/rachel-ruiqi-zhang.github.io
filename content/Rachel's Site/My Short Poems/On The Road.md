Fields of gold—
are they rapeseed flowers?
I lift my head and fall
straight into the sky,
blue collapsing into white,
low trees dissolving,
fields flickering...
Sunlight pouring.

My arm stings—
how can the prickling sensation be so similar?
Time folds into itself,
as space, too, spills across its seams.
The train rocks, relentlessly,
through past and present,
passing north and south,
carrying voices alive and voices once were—
into memory
and longing
as if they are the same.

金黄色的田野——
那是油菜花吗？
抬头
撞进眼睛里的
只有蓝天、白云、
状似低矮的树，
和闪烁着的田地
看不清细节
手臂传来刺刺痒痒的感觉
怎么会连刺痒都如此相同呢？
时间、空间，
哪里还有差别
火车晃晃，但不悠悠
驶过过去、现在，
驶过南方、北方，
驶过有人的地方和无人的地方，
驶过记忆和念想
