What to think of?
Bees weaved their nets;
Clouds sprout from the earth;
Eight-legged birdlings
tumbling round and round and round.
Think NO MORE—
A voice says,
dripping down the shoulder blades.
—None of it concerns you—
—None—

I stretched my antennae.
Whirling and whooshing,
Winds rush
and crash into the curtain.
What to think of?
What SHOULD be thought of?
What CAN be thought of?
The curtain nets the winds.
